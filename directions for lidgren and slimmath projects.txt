lidgren:

svn checkout http://lidgren-network-gen3.googlecode.com/svn/trunk/Lidgren.Network

into a new folder named

Lidgren.Network 

in this folder, then apply the lidgren_patch.patch to that folder.


SlimMath:

svn checkout http://slimmath.googlecode.com/svn/trunk/SlimMath

into a new folder named

SlimMath

in this folder, then apply the slimmath_patch.patch to that folder




After you are done, you should have at least the following folders:

Example
lib
Lidgren.Network
PNet
PNetC
PNetS
PNetU
SlimMath
Tests
